Why Learn with Andrew?

With years of experience in teaching and performance, Andrew provides personalized lessons tailored to your skill level.

Andrew Shaw is a dedicated pianist and cellist with years of experience in both performance and teaching. He has been a member of the Northern Tier Symphony Orchestra from 2017 to 2020 and currently plays with the Deseret Symphony Orchestra since 2024. In addition to his orchestra work, Andrew volunteers by performing in churches, using his musical talents to bring people together. With a focus on personalized lessons, Andrew tailors his teaching to each student’s needs, helping them build confidence and skill in a supportive environment. Whether you're a beginner or advanced, Andrew’s approach ensures a rewarding musical journey.
