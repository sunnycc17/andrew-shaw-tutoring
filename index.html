My Teaching Philosophy

I focus on the core elements of music, ensuring that students build a strong foundation while developing creativity and adaptability. Improvisation is a key tool I use to help students internalize patterns and tackle difficult passages. By combining structured learning with exploratory techniques, students gain both technical proficiency and musical confidence.

Core Elements of My Teaching

  • Rhythm Mastery Developing a strong internal pulse and a feel for various rhythms.
  • Ear Training Strengthening the ability to recognize and reproduce various patterns in music.
  • Basic Music Theory Developing an understanding of intervals and basic chord/scale construction.
  • Technique & Biomechanics Using efficient movement to enhance control and prevent strain.
  • Improvisation as a Learning Tool Applying improvisation to reinforce patterns, technique, and musicality.
  • Personalized Instruction Adapting lessons and creating routines to fit each student’s unique goals and learning style.

Looking for an Affordable Instrument?

  • Facebook Marketplace is a great place to find free or inexpensive pianos.

  • Most churches are open to letting people practice on their pianos.

  • Electric keyboards are fine for beginners, but I recommend getting a full-size (88-key) weighted keyboard for the best learning experience.
